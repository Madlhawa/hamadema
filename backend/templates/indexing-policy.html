Search Indexing Policy

Hamadema operates strictly on the principles of fair use and public data indexing. As a vertical search engine focused on consumer electronics, we index publicly available data on the open web, much like standard web browsers and major search engines do.

Responsible Indexing Practices

We take the operational stability of the stores we index very seriously. Our automated indexing processes are designed to be respectful of server resources. We operate at controlled, responsible intervals and implement rate limiting to ensure our indexers never impact the performance, bandwidth, or availability of the websites we catalog.

Mutually Beneficial Exposure

Our goal is to organize the internet's tech products for consumers while acting as a free marketing channel for retailers. By including your public product listings in our search index, we provide your store with increased visibility and direct customer referrals. We send high-intent shoppers—users who are actively searching for the products you sell—directly to your checkout pages.

Contact and Opt-Out

We are committed to maintaining a positive relationship with all retailers in our network. If you are a store owner and would like to optimize how your products appear on Hamadema, update an outdated listing, or if you prefer that we do not index your public pages, please contact us at hello@hamadema.lk. We honor all indexing removal requests promptly.
