About Our Search Engine

Hamadema is Sri Lanka's premier technology search engine. We exist to solve a simple problem: finding the exact tech gear you need across dozens of different websites is incredibly time-consuming and difficult.

Our solution is a lightning-fast search engine that organizes the internet's electronics. We aggregate publicly available product data from across the web into a single, unified search experience. By centralizing this information, we help users find exactly what they are looking for in seconds, comparing prices and specifications instantly.

At the same time, we serve as a powerful, free discovery platform for retailers. By indexing their public product pages, we provide massive exposure and drive high-intent, ready-to-buy traffic directly to their websites.
